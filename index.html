TIPS

Wall jump between two close walls to climb up fast.
Chain dash + wall jump for maximum distance.
Slide into a jump to keep your momentum.
Look for checkpoints (glowing flags) in later levels.
Beat your ghost trail to improve your best time!
Collect golden orbs to buy skins in the Skins menu.
Chain moves quickly to build combos for achievements.
Try Practice Mode (in pause menu) to learn levels risk-free.
Daily Challenge gives you a new procedural level every day.
Endless Mode tests how far you can go before dying.

XP ABILITY UNLOCKS

Lv.3 (500 XP) — Ground Pound: slam down in midair
Lv.4 (800 XP) — Glide: hold jump while falling to slow descent
Lv.5 (1200 XP) — Double Jump: jump again in midair
Lv.6 (1700 XP) — Slow-Motion: press Q to slow time
Lv.7 (2400 XP) — Speed Burst: auto-activates at 10+ combo

BLOCK TYPES

TERRAIN

Platform — Solid ground to run on
Wall — Vertical surface for wall-jumping and sliding
Ice Platform — Slippery surface, momentum carries you
Moving Platform — Follows a set path back and forth
Falling Platform — Crumbles after you step on it
Disappearing Platform — Phases in and out on a timer
Magnetic Platform — Pulls you toward it when nearby

HAZARDS

Spikes — Instant death on contact
Laser Beam — Cycles on/off, deadly when active
Sawblade — Spinning circular blade, instant death
Wall Spike — Extends/retracts from walls on a timer
Lava Floor — Deadly molten surface with animated waves
Enemy Drone — Patrols and chases you when close

MOVEMENT BLOCKS

Boost Pad — Launches you forward at high speed
Bounce Pad — Springs you high into the air
Trampoline — Extra-high bounce with spring compression
Conveyor Belt — Moves you in one direction while standing on it
Speed Pad — Temporarily boosts your running speed
Spring Wall — Bounces you horizontally on contact
Zipline — Slide along a rope between two points

SPECIAL ZONES

Gravity Zone — Inverts gravity inside the zone
Wind Zone — Pushes you with constant force
Water Pool — Slows movement by 40% while inside
Teleporter — Warps you to a linked destination
Portal Beam — Horizontal teleport line to another location

INTERACTIVE

Checkpoint — Respawn point when activated
Orb — Collect for currency to buy skins
Crumbling Wall — Breaks after 2 hits (use ground pound)
Goal — Reach this to complete the level
