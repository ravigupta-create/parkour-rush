HOW TO WIN

WHERE TO FIND THINGS

Campaign (Levels 1-23): From the main menu, click PLAY to start from your current level, or click LEVEL SELECT to pick any unlocked level.
Boss Levels (21-23): Click LEVEL SELECT on the main menu, scroll down to levels 21, 22, and 23. You must beat level 20 first to unlock them.
Endless Mode: Click ENDLESS on the main menu. You run forever until you die.
Daily Challenge: Click DAILY on the main menu. A new unique level every day.
Time Attack: Click TIME ATTACK on the main menu. Race the clock on campaign levels.
Weekly Challenge: Click WEEKLY on the main menu. Special challenge that changes weekly.
Difficulty: On the level select screen, look for the difficulty buttons (EASY / MEDIUM / HARD / EXTREME) at the top. Click one to switch.
Practice Mode: While in a level, press ESC to pause, then click PRACTICE: OFF to toggle it on. You get infinite lives.
Skins & Shop: Click SKINS or SHOP on the main menu. Buy skins with orbs you collect in levels.
Controls Reference: Click CONTROLS on the main menu to see all keybinds.
Level Editor: Click LEVEL EDITOR on the main menu to build your own levels.
Stats & Achievements: Click STATS or ACHIEVEMENTS on the main menu.
Best Runs & Ghosts: Click BEST RUNS on the main menu to see your replays and ghost data.

BASICS

Your goal is to reach the pink GOAL zone at the end of each level.
Use A/D to move and W/Space to jump. Master these before anything else.
Start on EASY difficulty — go to LEVEL SELECT and pick EASY at the top.
Checkpoints (green flags) save your progress mid-level. Touch them!

MOVEMENT MASTERY

Wall jump is your best friend. Jump into a wall, then jump off it to climb.
Dash (SHIFT) in midair to cross big gaps. Save it for when you need it.
Slide (S/Down) under low ceilings with spikes on top.
At Lv.4 XP, hold W/Space while falling to GLIDE — extends your reach hugely. (Check your level in STATS.)
At Lv.5 XP, you get DOUBLE JUMP — press W/Space again in midair. Game changer.
Chain moves: wall jump → dash → double jump → glide for maximum distance.

LEVEL STRATEGIES

Levels 1-5 (click LEVEL SELECT → pick 1-5): Learn the basics. Jump, wall jump, dash, slide.
Levels 6-7: Wall spikes retract on a timer. Watch the pattern, then go.
Levels 8-10: Enemy drones patrol areas. Wait for them to move away, then sprint past.
Levels 11-13: New blocks appear — speed pads, sawblades, trampolines, disappearing platforms. Stay alert.
Levels 14-20: Drones + every obstacle type. Memorize patterns and be patient.
Boss 21 — Spike Wall (LEVEL SELECT → 21): Never stop moving right. The wall chases you. Jump and dash constantly.
Boss 22 — Rising Lava (LEVEL SELECT → 22): Climb fast. Wall jump between close walls to ascend before lava catches you.
Boss 23 — Laser Drone (LEVEL SELECT → 23): Watch for the laser warning line, then dodge sideways. Hit the boss when it pauses.

GETTING GOLD STARS

Gold = fast time AND zero deaths. Use Practice Mode (ESC → PRACTICE) to learn risk-free first.
Check your ghost trail in BEST RUNS (main menu) to race against your best time.
Dash off every boost pad at peak speed for fastest routes.
Skip orbs on speed runs — they slow you down with detours.
Slow-Motion (press Q at Lv.6 XP) helps with precise jumps in tough sections.

HAZARD GUIDE

Spikes: Jump over or slide under. Never touch them.
Lasers: They cycle on/off. Count the rhythm and run through during the off phase.
Drones: Red orbs that chase within 60px. Stay far or sprint past when they patrol away.
Sawblades: They spin in place. Time your jump to pass above or below.
Falling platforms: They crumble fast — jump immediately, don't wait.
Disappearing platforms: Watch the fade. Jump on when solid, off before they vanish.
Wind zones: Dash through headwind. Use tailwind for extra speed.
Ice: Momentum carries you. Jump early and don't overshoot edges.
Trampolines: Bounce you high. Aim your landing carefully.
Speed pads: Launch you forward fast. Be ready to jump at the end.
Lava floors: Instant death. Jump across using platforms above.
Magnetic platforms: Pull you toward them. Use the pull to reach tricky spots.

ENDLESS MODE TIPS

Click ENDLESS on the main menu to play. It gets harder every 300m with new hazards.
150m: water pools. 200m: wall spikes + speed pads. 250m: trampolines.
300m+: drones + magnetic platforms. 350m+: disappearing platforms.
400m+: sawblades. 450m+: lava floors. Stay calm and keep moving right.
Save your dash for emergencies — falling into a gap with no platform below.

XP & PROGRESSION

Complete levels to earn XP. Gold stars give the most (+100 bonus). Check your XP in STATS (main menu).
Zero death completions give +75 bonus XP.
Build combos (quick successive moves) for extra XP.
Unlock abilities at Lv.3-8 that make later levels much easier. See CONTROLS → XP Ability Unlocks.
Collect orbs during levels to buy skins — go to SHOP on the main menu.
Daily challenges (DAILY on main menu) give unique procedural levels — great XP practice.
